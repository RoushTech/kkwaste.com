At K & K Waste Disposal there are only (3) three holidays that our trucks do not operate on. These holidays are as follows: Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day and New Years day. If your normal service day falls on one of these holidays we will list your alternate service day on this website. Any route changes due to a holiday will also be on our answering service for our customers convenience. Feel free to call our office at anytime if you have a question concerning your service day.

Holiday Schedules

Thanksgiving/Christmas/New Years Day: These holidays fall on different days of the week. Your service day will be on the day after these holidays.
